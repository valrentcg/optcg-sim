# v1.0.15

Friends get social — chat, game invites, and friend requests — plus an installer fix.

## Friends & social

- **Chat with your friends.** Click a friend in your list to open a conversation. Messages are saved server-side, so you can scroll back and read anything sent while you were offline.
- **Invite friends to a game.** Hit **Invite** on an online friend to spin up a private room and drop them a game invite; they get a pop-up to **Accept** and join you straight in the lobby.
- **Friend-request pop-ups.** When someone adds you, a card slides in on the side of the screen — **Accept**, **Decline**, or **Ignore** (block) right there, without opening the Friends page.
- **Add your opponent after a match.** If you just played someone who isn't already your friend, there's an **Add Friend** button on the end-of-match screen.
- **A "Refresh" button on the Friends list** re-pulls your friends from the server, so a list that came up empty repopulates.

## In-match chat & combat log

- **Copy from chat and the combat log.** New **Copy** buttons on the match chat and the combat log put the full conversation / history on your clipboard; you can paste into the chat as usual.

## Installer

- **Fixed "Install Partially Succeeded."** New installs no longer show the scary "some steps may have failed" dialog at the end — the setup now finishes cleanly.

*(Reminder: a friend request is two-way — the other person still has to press **Accept** before you become friends.)*

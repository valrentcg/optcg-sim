## v1.0.8 — Ranked & Casual Matchmaking (Bug Testing Season)

Competitive play arrives in a **Bug Testing Season** — it's in dev, so expect rough edges.

### Matchmaking
- **Ranked** and **Casual** are now their own auto-queues. Pick a deck, hit **Queue Match**, and you're paired with an opponent **near your skill** (the range widens the longer you wait).
- **Ready check** — when a match is found, both players get an Accept/Decline prompt with a countdown, League-style. No lobby codes, no browsing: accept and you're dropped straight into the match.
- Ranked results move your bounty; Casual doesn't. Custom Rooms are unchanged and separate.

### The Bounty ladder
- A themed 9-tier ladder from **Apprentice → Yonko**, with **Pirate King** reserved for the very top — capped at Gol D. Roger's real ฿5,564,800,000.
- A hidden skill rating matches you; your visible **Bounty** is what you climb, with win-streak **Rampage** bonuses and a **Vivre Card** that can save you from a demotion at lower tiers.
- Results are settled **server-side** so they can't be faked.

### Most Wanted
- A new **Most Wanted** leaderboard on the main menu — the sea's most notorious pirates, ranked by bounty.

### Profile & Match History
- Overview now shows your **real bounty & tier**, and your win rate **excludes bot games**.
- Recent matches are tagged by type (**Ranked / Casual / Custom / Bot**) and are **clickable** into full match detail.
- **Deck History** can be split by **Casual** vs **Ranked**.
- In a match's **decklists**, **hover a card** to preview it, and use **Save Deck** to copy a list straight into one of your deck slots.

_Returning players update automatically._

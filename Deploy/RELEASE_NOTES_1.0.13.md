# v1.0.13

Polish and fixes from live play.

**Board & visuals**

- **Blocker shield** — the glow now hugs the shield icon (no more big square), and the shield sits on the **center-facing side** of the board for both players.
- **Attached DON!!** now group neatly within a character's width, and spread out to fit the card's width when the character is **rested** (sideways).
- **Field cards and their attached DON!!** now render **above** the board zones (no more DON tucking behind the Stage zone).
- **End-of-match "View Board"** now reveals **both** players' remaining hand and Life.

**Gameplay feel**

- The **AI pauses before it counters**, so the counter reads as a clear, distinct action instead of the power snapping up the instant you attack.

**Bug fixes**

- The **AI's card search** no longer briefly flashes its cards to you.
- A **rested character returned to hand** now sits upright like every other card.
- The **Luffy & Ace Leader (ST30-001)** no longer wrongly gives *itself* +3000 on the opponent's turn — that buff is for your [Portgas.D.Ace] / [Monkey.D.Luffy] Characters, not the Leader.
